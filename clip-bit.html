clip:bit Project

Enhancing outdoor learning with persistent data logging for the BBC micro:bit.

Key Features

Outdoor Data Logging

Designed for field trips and surveys, the clip:bit enables students to collect environmental data efficiently.

User-Friendly Interface

Equipped with LED displays for daylight readability and up to 12 classification categories.

Persistent Storage

Data is stored persistently on the device, ensuring information is retained until uploaded.

Smart Data Upload

Intelligent scanning of data collection sheets and seamless upload to class databases.

Educational Applications

  • Facilitates cross-curricular outdoor learning, integrating computing with environmental studies.
  • Encourages student engagement through hands-on data collection and analysis.
  • Supports the development of digital literacy and scientific inquiry skills.
  • Enables visualization of data through the Classroom Cloudlet, fostering discussions on data ownership and privacy.
